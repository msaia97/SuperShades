Super Shades

This is a data visualization of Marvel supers and their primary costume color. The large circle represents the supers’ main color and the color of the small circle shows the corresponding supers’ secondary color, if applicable. Each character bubble is outlined with either red or blue, depending on if the character is a villain or hero.
